What the wizard does

Six focused steps — takes about 10 minutes.

01
Describe Your Brand
Name, purpose, unique value, color palette with pickers, logo upload, and current site URL.
02
Add Inspiration Sites
Add up to 3 sites you love. Capture full-page screenshots or upload your own.
03
Distill Your Aesthetic
Pick your overall style, describe your audience and the emotion you want to convey.
04
Deep Dive Analysis
Checkboxes + notes for typography, layout, colors, imagery, and motion — per site. Grab specific regions with the built-in screen capper.
05
Review Your Brief
See everything assembled into a proof before generating. Edit any section inline.
06
Get Your Prompts
PDF design brief + 3 levels of Claude Design prompts — quick prototype, full design system, and complete spec.

Common questions

Is my data private?
Your session is stored locally in your browser. When you click Generate, your brief is sent to our server to call Claude — we don't store it. Screenshots never leave your browser until you explicitly download the PDF.
Do I need an account?
No account needed to run the wizard. An account is only required to save sessions to the cloud dashboard — the free tier is enough.
Why isn't it on the Chrome Web Store?
SitePrompt is currently in preview. Loading unpacked takes 30 seconds and keeps you on the latest version. A Web Store release is planned.
What do I do with the Claude Design prompts?
Copy any of the three prompts and paste it into claude.ai/design. The quick prompt generates a landing page prototype in under a minute.
